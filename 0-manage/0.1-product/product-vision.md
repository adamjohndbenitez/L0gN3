📌 Product Vision Summary

LifeLogMan / Stealthagon / Artilligenie is a personal, privacy‑first life‑logging ecosystem that blends resilient microservices architecture, enterprise‑grade cybersecurity, and AI‑driven intelligence. Inspired by real experiences—from immigrating to a new country, managing sensitive data, and recovering from a scam—this platform turns personal data into secure, actionable insights.
